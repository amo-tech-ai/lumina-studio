# Creative Director

Creative direction for fashion shoots and campaigns — concept, mood, and rationale **before**
the shot list and production plan. Pairs with the campaign system's Creative Director Agent
(`docs/prd/campaign-prd.md`) and feeds `shot-list-generator`.

**Use when:** kicking off a campaign or shoot, defining a creative concept, building a moodboard
rationale, choosing a visual direction / color story / styling angle from Brand DNA, or evaluating
competing creative concepts before committing to production.

## Inputs → Outputs
- **Inputs:** Brand DNA (voice, palette, audience), campaign goal, products, season, references.
- **Outputs:** creative concept(s), mood/visual direction, color story, styling angle, rationale,
  and a hand-off brief for `shot-list-generator` + the capture/styling references.

## Methodologies (diverge → converge)
- **SIT** (Systematic Inventive Thinking) · **TRIZ** (inventive problem solving) ·
  **Bisociation** (cross-domain matrix) · **SCAMPER** (Substitute/Combine/Adapt/Modify/Put-to-use/
  Eliminate/Reverse) · **Synectics** (analogical thinking).

## 3-axis evaluation (score concepts before committing)
| Axis | Criteria |
|------|----------|
| **Cannes** | Creativity, originality, execution quality |
| **D&AD** | Design excellence, craft, cultural impact |
| **HumanKind** | Emotional resonance, behavioral change, purpose |

## 5-phase process
1. **Brief** — challenge, audience, constraints (from campaign goal + Brand DNA).
2. **Research** — context, competitors, cultural signals.
3. **Ideation** — apply methodologies in sequence; diverge then converge.
4. **Evaluation** — score on the 3 axes; refine top concepts.
5. **Hand-off** — rationale + moodboard + execution direction → `shot-list-generator`, then
   `styling/` + `capture/` references.
