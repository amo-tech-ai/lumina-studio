# Product Photography — Photographer Brief Specs

## When to Use This Skill

Use when you need to:
- Define shot requirements for a real product photographer
- Specify camera, lighting, and composition per shot type
- Build the product shot section of a production package
- Create QC pass/fail criteria for uploaded product shots
- Match product photography to brand DNA and channel requirements

## Core Principle

**This skill briefs real photographers. It does NOT generate AI images.** Every output is a document — a shot spec, an equipment list, a QC checklist — that a human photographer reads and executes on set.

## The 5 Product Shot Types

### Type 1: Clean Product Shot

**Purpose:** E-commerce listings, product pages, catalogs — the "money shot"
**Channel:** Shopify primary, Amazon main image

```
CLEAN PRODUCT SHOT SPEC:

Camera:
  Body:       Full-frame DSLR or mirrorless
  Lens:       50mm or 85mm prime (minimal distortion)
  Aperture:   f/8 to f/11 (maximum sharpness, full DOF)
  Shutter:    1/125s (sync speed with strobes)
  ISO:        100 (lowest noise)
  White bal:  5500K (flash preset)

Lighting:
  Key:        Softbox 120cm, 10 o'clock, 45° above, 3ft from product
  Fill:       White reflector, 2 o'clock, same height as product
  Background: 2x strip softbox aimed at seamless, +1 stop over key
  Ratio:      1.5:1 (very flat, minimal shadow)
  Color temp: 5500K neutral (no gels)

Composition:
  Framing:    Product fills 85% of frame (Amazon requirement)
  Position:   Dead center, symmetrical
  Angle:      Straight-on (eye-level with product center)
  Crop:       Square (1:1) for ecommerce, 4:5 for Instagram
  Headroom:   Equal padding all sides

Background:
  Surface:    White seamless paper (2.72m roll) OR light tent
  Target:     Pure white (RGB 255,255,255) after post
  Alternative: Brand-specified color per DNA

Set Requirements:
  □ Product table or plinth (correct height for lens)
  □ White seamless paper or light tent
  □ Product cleaned (no dust, fingerprints, tags tucked)
  □ Steam garments before shooting
  □ Alignment tools (ruler, grid overlay in viewfinder)

QC Pass Criteria:
  □ Product sharp edge-to-edge (no soft corners)
  □ White balance neutral (grey card reference shot taken)
  □ No color cast from environment
  □ Product fills minimum 85% of frame
  □ No visible dust, threads, or fingerprints
  □ Background evenly lit (no gradients unless specified)
  □ Consistent exposure across all shots in series
```

### Type 2: Lifestyle Shot

**Purpose:** Social media, marketing, brand storytelling
**Channel:** Instagram, TikTok, website hero banners

```
LIFESTYLE SHOT SPEC:

Camera:
  Body:       Full-frame DSLR or mirrorless
  Lens:       35mm (environmental) or 50mm (natural perspective)
  Aperture:   f/2.8 to f/4 (subject focus, environment blur)
  Shutter:    1/200s+ (freeze movement if model)
  ISO:        Auto (up to 1600 acceptable on location)
  White bal:  Auto or Kelvin match to ambient

Lighting:
  Primary:    Natural light (window, outdoor)
  Fill:       White reflector opposite window/sun
  Backup:     Portable LED panel (bicolor) for cloudy days
  Avoid:      Direct harsh sunlight (use diffusion panel)
  Best time:  Overcast day OR golden hour (30 min before sunset)

Composition:
  Framing:    Product in context — model using/wearing in environment
  Rule:       Rule of thirds (product at intersection point)
  Angle:      Eye-level or slightly above (natural perspective)
  Crop:       4:5 portrait (Instagram max), 9:16 (stories/reels)
  Depth:      Show environment — don't isolate product

Scene Requirements:
  □ Location scouted and approved (matches brand DNA)
  □ Props list prepared (see lifestyle context table below)
  □ Backup indoor location identified (weather contingency)
  □ Model booked if needed (see casting brief)
  □ Permits obtained if public location

QC Pass Criteria:
  □ Product clearly visible and identifiable
  □ Environment supports brand story (not distracting)
  □ Natural, unforced moment (not stiff catalog pose)
  □ Lighting feels natural (no obvious artificial light)
  □ No brand-competing signage or logos in background
  □ Color palette matches brand DNA
```

**Lifestyle Context Table:**

| Context | Location | Props | Model Direction | Best For |
|---------|----------|-------|----------------|----------|
| Urban | Café, street, rooftop | Coffee, phone, tote bag | Walking, sitting, looking away | Streetwear, casual, accessories |
| Casual | Home, park, neighborhood | Book, blanket, pet | Relaxed, morning routine | Knitwear, loungewear, basics |
| Active | Trail, gym, beach | Water bottle, bike, yoga mat | Mid-activity, movement | Activewear, outerwear, footwear |
| Professional | Office, co-work, transit | Laptop, briefcase, coffee | Purposeful walk, working | Suiting, workwear, bags |
| Social | Restaurant, gallery, event | Wine glass, clutch | Laughing, conversing | Evening wear, jewelry, formal |

### Type 3: Hero Shot

**Purpose:** Landing pages, campaign headers, featured content
**Channel:** Website hero banner, ad creative, lookbook cover

```
HERO SHOT SPEC:

Camera:
  Body:       Full-frame or medium format (max resolution)
  Lens:       85mm (product isolation) or 70-200mm (compression)
  Aperture:   f/2.8 to f/4 (dramatic shallow DOF)
  Shutter:    1/125s (strobe sync)
  ISO:        100
  White bal:  5500K base, adjust per brand DNA

Lighting:
  Key:        Strip softbox 30x120cm, 9 o'clock, 30° above, 2ft
  Fill:       V-flat (black side = negative fill for drama)
              OR V-flat (white side = softer fill)
  Background: Dark grey/black seamless, no light (-2 stops)
              OR gradient (spot aimed at bg center)
  Accent:     Rim light, 5 o'clock, behind product, 1/4 power
  Ratio:      4:1 (dramatic shadow) or 2:1 (polished)
  Color temp: 5500K with optional +200K warm shift for luxury

Composition:
  Framing:    Product as hero — dramatic, confident placement
  Position:   Off-center (rule of thirds) or centered with symmetry
  Angle:      Low angle (looking up = power/premium)
              OR 45° (classic product beauty angle)
  Crop:       16:9 (hero banner), 2:3 (portrait campaign)
  Negative:   Generous space for text overlay areas

Set Requirements:
  □ Seamless paper or styled surface (per brand DNA)
  □ Product in pristine condition (hero = perfect specimen)
  □ Fog machine or atmosphere if specified (editorial mood)
  □ Props minimal — product is sole focus
  □ Tethered shooting (check focus on large monitor)

QC Pass Criteria:
  □ Product is the undeniable focal point
  □ Lighting creates dimension and mood
  □ Shadow direction consistent with brand DNA (dramatic/soft)
  □ Resolution sufficient for large format (minimum 4000px wide)
  □ Space available for text overlay without covering product
  □ Color grading matches brand DNA target
```

### Type 4: Detail Shot

**Purpose:** Showcasing quality, craftsmanship, material details
**Channel:** Amazon secondary images, product page zoom, social carousel

```
DETAIL SHOT SPEC:

Camera:
  Body:       Full-frame DSLR or mirrorless
  Lens:       100mm macro (1:1 magnification capability)
  Aperture:   f/8 to f/11 (balance sharpness and DOF)
  Shutter:    1/125s (use tripod to avoid shake)
  ISO:        100
  Focus:      Manual focus with focus peaking OR focus stacking

Lighting:
  Key:        Ring light (even, shadowless on small areas)
              OR softbox 60cm very close to subject
  Fill:       White card reflector underneath/opposite
  Background: Natural product surface or neutral backdrop
  Ratio:      1.5:1 (flat, accurate — this is documentation)
  Color temp: 5500K neutral (color accuracy critical)

Composition:
  Framing:    Tight crop on specific detail
  Focus:      Sharpest point on key detail (stitching, hardware, texture)
  Angle:      20-45° (dimensional, not flat perpendicular)
  Crop:       1:1 (square) or 4:3 (detail callout)

Equipment:
  □ Tripod (mandatory — no handheld macro)
  □ Focus rail (for precise focus adjustment)
  □ Remote shutter release (eliminate camera shake)
  □ Grey card (color accuracy reference)
  □ Compressed air (remove dust before shooting)

What to Capture (Checklist per Product):
  □ Stitching at key seams (collar, cuff, hem)
  □ Hardware close-ups (buttons, zippers, clasps, buckles)
  □ Fabric/material texture (weave, grain, finish)
  □ Label/brand mark (if visible from outside)
  □ Lining detail (if premium lining is a selling point)
  □ Sole/bottom detail (footwear)
  □ Closure mechanism (clasp, magnetic, snap)

Three-Level Material Spec (for QC):
  Level 1 — Base:         "leather" / "cotton" / "metal"
  Level 2 — Construction: "full-grain" / "twill weave" / "brushed nickel"
  Level 3 — Surface:      "smooth matte" / "stone-washed" / "polished"

QC Pass Criteria:
  □ Key detail is tack-sharp (check at 100% zoom)
  □ Color accurate to physical product (grey card calibrated)
  □ Three-level material clearly visible
  □ No added dust, threads, or artifacts
  □ Angle shows dimension (not flat perpendicular)
  □ Consistent lighting across all detail shots in series
```

### Type 5: Flat Lay

**Purpose:** Social media, styled content, collection overview
**Channel:** Instagram grid, Pinterest, lookbook spreads

```
FLAT LAY SPEC:

Camera:
  Body:       Full-frame DSLR or mirrorless
  Lens:       35mm or 50mm (on copy stand/boom arm overhead)
  Aperture:   f/5.6 to f/8 (even sharpness across flat plane)
  Shutter:    1/125s
  ISO:        100
  Mount:      Overhead boom arm or copy stand (camera pointing straight down)

Lighting:
  Key:        Large softbox 150cm, directly overhead, 2ft above surface
  Fill:       White surface below (bounces light back into shadows)
  Alternative: Large window (north-facing) from one side + reflector
  Ratio:      1:1 (extremely flat, no shadows)
  Color temp: 5500K neutral

Composition:
  Framing:    Overhead, perfectly perpendicular to surface
  Layout:     Styled arrangement — hero product center, supporting items around
  Spacing:    Consistent gaps between items (use ruler/grid)
  Crop:       1:1 (Instagram), 4:5 (Instagram max), 2:3 (Pinterest)
  Rule:       Odd numbers of items (3, 5, 7) look more natural

Styling Surface Options:
  □ White marble tile (luxury, clean)
  □ Light wood (natural, warm)
  □ Linen fabric (organic, texture)
  □ Concrete slab (modern, industrial)
  □ Colored paper (brand-specific)

Props per Context:
  Fashion:    Sunglasses, watch, jewelry, wallet, scarf
  Beauty:     Flowers, greenery, cotton pads, small bottles
  Food:       Utensils, napkin, ingredients, cutting board
  Tech:       Earbuds, cable, notebook, pen
  Lifestyle:  Coffee cup, book, candle, keys

QC Pass Criteria:
  □ Camera perfectly level (no perspective distortion)
  □ All items sharp (consistent focus plane)
  □ Spacing even and intentional
  □ No harsh shadows (lighting is flat)
  □ Surface clean and styled
  □ Hero product is clearly the focal point
  □ Props complement but don't compete
```

## Brand DNA → Product Shot Translation

| DNA Direction | Key Light | Background | Mood | Ratio | Post-Production |
|--------------|-----------|------------|------|-------|-----------------|
| Warm / Luxury | Large soft, warm shift (+200K) | Cream, marble, gold accents | Intimate, rich | 2:1 | Warm grade, lifted shadows |
| Cool / Modern | Medium hard, cool shift | White, grey, minimal | Clean, sharp | 3:1 | Cool grade, full blacks |
| Neutral / Clean | Large soft, daylight | Pure white | Accurate, clear | 1.5:1 | Neutral, minimal grading |
| Dramatic / Editorial | Strip box, hard light | Dark grey/black | Bold, confident | 4:1 | High contrast, deep shadows |
| Natural / Organic | Window light, overcast | Natural surface (wood, linen) | Warm, authentic | 2:1 | Muted, earthy tones |
