# Research Task: Design an AI-Native Production Operations & Booking System

## Objective

Design a complete end-to-end production management platform for photography, video, ecommerce, fashion, and advertising projects.

The system should manage the entire lifecycle from customer discovery to final delivery, publishing, payments, and performance tracking.

The AI should act as an Operations Manager, Producer, Project Manager, Creative Director, Resource Planner, and Logistics Coordinator.

## Goal

Build a production operating system that coordinates customers, creative teams, resources, schedules, budgets, logistics, asset production, approvals, payments, and automated publishing. The platform should ensure every stakeholder knows what to do, when to do it, what resources are needed, how they will be paid, and how final assets flow automatically to ecommerce stores, marketplaces, social platforms, and publishing tools such as Postiz, Shopify, Amazon, Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and Pinterest.
