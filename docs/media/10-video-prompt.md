# Research Task: Build a Video Intelligence Knowledge Base

## Objective

Research and build a comprehensive Video Intelligence Knowledge Base for AI agents used by FashionOS/iPix.

The goal is to enable AI agents to recommend the best video formats for marketing, ecommerce, advertising, education, branding, and social media.

Use official platform documentation first. Supplement with trusted industry sources. Cite every source.
