# StudioDock Feature Catalog

**Website:** [StudioDock](https://www.getstudiodock.com?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

## Executive Summary

StudioDock is a specialized booking platform for:

* Photography studios
* Content studios
* Creative spaces
* Podcast studios
* Cyclorama studios
* Product photography studios

Unlike Calendly or Acuity, StudioDock is built specifically for **studio rentals**, where customers book:

* Rooms
* Equipment
* Lighting
* Backdrops
* Staff assistance
* Time blocks
