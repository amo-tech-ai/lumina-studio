## 🧩 PAGE 1 — CALENDAR (Dashboard Integration)

**Purpose:**
Give designers and organizers a clear view of all upcoming events, fittings, and bookings in a calendar format.

**Component Name:** `CalendarPage`

### **Layout & Content**

* **Header Bar:**

  * Title: “Calendar”
  * Quick Filter: “All | Events | Bookings | Deadlines | Meetings”
  * Button: “+ New Event” (accent color)
* **Month Grid (Full Width):**

  * Calendar cards color-coded by type:

    * Events → Purple
    * Fittings/Bookings → Pink
    * Meetings → Gray
  * Each item includes: event title, time, team avatars, location tooltip.
* **Right Drawer (on click):**

  * Event banner image
  * Date/time, location, assigned team, and notes
  * CTA: “View Event Details”
* **Tagline / Copy Example:**

  > “Stay perfectly in sync — from runway rehearsals to studio fittings.”

**Image Usage:**
Hero calendar image or blurred background of a designer planning schedule.

---

## 🧩 PAGE 2 — BOOKINGS (Studio & Client Management)

**Purpose:**
Centralize all studio reservations, collaborations, and brand appointments.

**Component Name:** `BookingsPage`

### **Layout & Content**

* **Header Bar:**

  * Title: “Bookings”
  * Search Field: “Search bookings, clients, or studios…”
  * Button: “+ Add Booking”
* **Filters:**

  * Date | Client | Venue | Status (Confirmed / Pending / Completed)
* **Booking Cards (Grid or Table View):**

  * Image thumbnail (designer or venue)
  * Booking Title (e.g., “Studio Session – Nova Atelier”)
  * Date, time, duration
  * Assigned staff/designer avatars
  * Status badge color-coded:

    * Green = Confirmed
    * Yellow = Pending
    * Gray = Completed
  * CTA: “View Details”
* **Empty State:**

  > “No bookings yet. Start by adding your next creative session.”

  * Button: “Add Booking”
* **Right Drawer (on click):**

  * Booking details, contact info, location map, and attachments.

**Tagline / Copy Example:**

> “Manage your creative sessions, fittings, and client appointments with ease.”

**Image Usage:**
Photos of studios, fabric swatches, and workspaces for background visuals.

## 🧩 PAGE 3 — DESIGNER LISTING & DESIGNER DETAILS

**Purpose:**
Provide a directory and detail pages for designers within the FashionOS ecosystem.

**Components:**

* `DesignerListingPage`
* `DesignerDetailPage`

---

### **Designer Listing Page**

**Layout & Content:**

* **Header Bar:**

  * Title: “Designers Directory”
  * Search: “Find designer or brand…”
  * Filters: Country | Category | Availability
* **Grid Layout:**

  * Each card shows:

    * Designer portrait
    * Name, brand, city
    * “View Profile” CTA
  * Hover state: short tagline (e.g., “Runway & Editorial Specialist”).
* **Tagline:**

  > “Meet the creative minds shaping the future of fashion.”
* **CTA:** “Add New Designer” (if admin).

**Image Usage:**
Clean portraits of designers with soft gradient backgrounds or monochrome overlays.

---

### **Designer Detail Page**

**Layout & Content:**

* **Header Section:**

  * Designer cover image
  * Profile photo + name, title, brand logo
  * Buttons: “Edit Profile” (admin), “Book Session” (organizer)
* **Stats Bar:**

  * Events Hosted | Collaborations | Collections | Rating (⭐ 4.8)
* **Tabs Section:**

  * **Portfolio:** Grid of images/videos from collections.
  * **Events:** List of hosted or upcoming events.
  * **Bookings:** History with clients and studios.
  * **Reviews:** Short feedback quotes and ratings.
* **CTA at bottom:** “Start Collaboration.”
* **Copy Example:**

  > “Capturing the intersection of art and design, [Designer Name] brings every project to life with purpose, creativity, and detail.”

**Image Usage:**
Portfolio grid with campaign visuals, behind-the-scenes photos, and product close-ups.

## 🧠 Visual & UX Consistency

* Same **sidebar navigation** as your existing dashboard.
* Use **pastel purple (#A87DF1)** for active states, hover accents, and CTAs.
* Maintain **rounded cards (12 px)** and **soft shadows (#00000010)**.
* Use **Inter** for body text, **Playfair Display / DM Serif** for section headers.
* Integrate team avatars, brand logos, and small icons for personality.
* Add hover animations and scroll fade-ins for polish.
