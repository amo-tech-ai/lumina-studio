## ✨ **Lovable Full Prompt: FashionOS Website UI/UX Design (Section-by-Section)**

**Goal:**
Design a **modern, editorial-inspired UI** for **FashionOS**, an AI-powered fashion platform.
Each section should feel elegant, minimal, and immersive — inspired by **Blend Studios’ visual rhythm** (split columns, image grids, clean typography, and precise spacing).

---

### 🎨 **Overall Visual Direction**

* **Style:** Editorial-tech hybrid — clean grids, soft rounded corners, balanced white space.
* **Layout rhythm:** Use a mix of split layouts, two-column storytelling, and image mosaics.
* **Imagery:** Use bright, high-contrast fashion photos with clean backgrounds.
* **Tone:** Confident, luxurious, creative — “Where fashion meets intelligent design.”
* **Interactions:** Smooth fade-ins, hover zoom, and scroll-based reveals.
* **Components:** Rounded cards, subtle shadows, elegant text blocks, full-width CTAs.

---

### 🧩 **Section 1 — Hero (“Exceptional Imagery, Every Time.”)**

**Layout:**

* Left: Large headline, 2–3 sentence intro, primary CTA (“Explore Designers”).
* Right: Masonry grid of fashion imagery (portrait + product + runway shots).
* Rounded corners (8–12px), 3-column grid, even spacing (24px gutters).

**Visuals:**

* Clean pastel background (cream/white).
* Images use soft shadows and slight color pop.

**Interactions:**

* Hover = subtle zoom (scale 1.03) + shadow.
* Scroll reveal: left text slides up, grid fades in.

---

### 🧩 **Section 2 — “A Studio You Can Trust” / About**

**Layout:**

* Split 50/50: Left text, Right large product photo.
* Title: “A Platform You Can Trust.”
* Subtext: Describe FashionOS as the trusted space for creators, photographers, and designers.
* Add soft background gradient (lavender → blush).

**Design Details:**

* Text box uses padding 64px, rounded container edges (16px).
* Shadow under image for subtle lift.

**Interactions:**

* Image parallax as user scrolls.
* Text fade-in from left.

---

### 🧩 **Section 3 — Services Carousel**

**Goal:** Show available creative services (Photography, Lookbooks, Video, Styling, PR, Influencers).

**Layout:**

* Full-width horizontal scroll (desktop) / vertical stack (mobile).
* 3–4 service cards visible per frame.
* Each card:

  * Image (top, 3:2 ratio)
  * Title (bold serif)
  * Short paragraph
  * “Book Now” button.

**Design Notes:**

* Cards: 12px radius, light shadow, pastel background.
* Hover: Elevate + highlight border.

**Interactions:**

* Scroll reveal (slide from bottom).
* Horizontal drag scroll on desktop.

---

### 🧩 **Section 4 — Marketplace / E-Commerce**

**Goal:** Integrate with Shopify marketplace.

**Layout:**

* Two-column:

  * Left: Product photo or mockup (e.g., accessories, looks).
  * Right: Title “Shop the Runway” + product highlights + “Shop Now” button.
* Background: subtle neutral gradient.

**Design Details:**

* Rounded product cards with pricing overlay.
* Include badges (Amazon, Shopify, Mercado Libre, etc.).
* Use elegant minimal icons.

**Interactions:**

* Product hover: zoom-in and “Add to Cart” overlay.
* Lazy-load product images.

---

### 🧩 **Section 5 — Event Planner + Venue Directory**

**Goal:** Let users browse or book fashion show venues.

**Layout:**

* Grid of venue cards (3 per row desktop, 1–2 mobile).
* Each card: Venue photo, name, city, capacity, “Book Venue” button.
* Above grid: Search + filter bar (AI search powered by CopilotKit).

**Design Notes:**

* Cards: rounded 16px, gradient accent overlay on hover.
* Use location icons (pin, calendar, people).

**Interactions:**

* Hover → dim background + highlight CTA.
* Filter chips animate smoothly.

---

**Goal:** Showcase AI features for fashion event automation.

**Layout:**

* Left: Chat mockup (AI planning a fashion event).
* Right: Text describing CopilotKit’s capabilities:

  * “Plan a runway show.”
  * “Find photographers near Paris.”
  * “Auto-publish product shots.”

**Visuals:**

* Gradient background (lavender to blush).
* Chat bubble mock interface, rounded with shadows.

**Interactions:**

* Typing animation for AI responses.
* Glow animation behind Copilot icon.

---

### 🧩 **Section 7 — Testimonials**

  > “FashionOS made it effortless to organize our fashion week showcase.”

  * — Designer Name, Paris Fashion House

### 🧩 **Section 8 — Call to Action**

**Goal:** End with a powerful join CTA.

**Layout:**

* Title: “Join the Future of Fashion.”
* Subtext: “Create your profile, connect, and showcase your work.”
* Two buttons: “Join Now” / “Explore Designers.”

**Design Notes:**

* Center-aligned, full-width gradient background.
* Buttons use brand accent colors.

**Interactions:**

* Hover pulse animation on main CTA.
* Subtle background shimmer effect.

---

### 🧩 **Section 9 — Footer**

**Layout:**

* Four-column grid:

  1. Brand logo + tagline.
  2. Menu links.
  3. Social media icons (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn).
  4. Newsletter field (rounded input, Subscribe button).

**Design Notes:**

* Black background, white text.
* Rounded corners for input.
* Use clear spacing (32px gutters).

---

### 🧱 **Design System & Tokens**

* **Corner radius:** 8–16px.
* **Grid gutters:** 24px desktop, 16px mobile.
* **Section padding:** 80px top/bottom desktop, 48px mobile.
* **Typography:**

  * Headings: Playfair Display (serif, 700).
  * Body: Inter (sans, 400–500).
* **Color palette:**

  * Cream `#FAFAFA`
  * Blush Pink `#FADADD`
  * Lavender `#EBDCFB`
  * Charcoal `#1F1F1F`
  * Orchid `#C67BF4`
