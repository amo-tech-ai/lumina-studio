1. **Hero Section**

   * Headline: “Create. Connect. Convert.”
   * Subtext: “End-to-end creative and digital services for brands that want to stand out.”
   * CTA Buttons: “Start a Project” and “View Portfolio”

       * Visual: Behind-the-scenes shoot photo or short video loop.
     * 📸 **Photography**

       * Description: Editorial, e-commerce, and event photography with post-production.
       * Visual: Gallery preview or photo grid hover effect.
     * 💻 **Web Design**

       * Description: Custom websites built for style and performance.

5. **Call-to-Action Section**

6. **Footer**

   * Links: Services, About, Portfolio, Contact
   * Social icons (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn)
   * Contact info and WhatsApp quick connect button.

---

### 🎨 **Visual & UX Style Guidelines**

* **Design Style:** Modern, elegant, and editorial — similar to creative studios like *Blend Studios* or *Fiverr Pro*.
* **Color Palette:**

  * Primary: `#EBDCFB` (lavender pastel)
  * Accent: `#FADADD` (blush)
  * Dark Text: `#1A1A1A`
  * Background: `#FFFFFF` / light neutral
* **Typography:**

  * Headlines: *Playfair Display* (serif, 48–64px)
  * Body: *Inter* or *Outfit* (sans-serif, 16–18px)
  * Buttons: Bold, all caps, letter-spacing 1.2px
* **Image Treatment:**

  * Rounded corners (8–12px)
  * Hover effects (zoom or color overlay)
* **Spacing:**

  * Section padding: 80px desktop, 48px mobile
  * Column gap: 32px desktop, 16px mobile
* **Grid System:** 12-column responsive layout

---

### 📱 **Responsive Design Rules**

* **Desktop (≥1200px):** Two-column or 3-card layouts.
* **Tablet (≥768px):** Collapse to 2-column grids.
* **Mobile (≤480px):** Stack sections vertically, single column.
* Sticky top navbar for easy navigation.

---

### ⚙️ **UX & Interaction Best Practices**

* Keep each service visually distinct (unique image or color accent).
* Add smooth **scroll-based animations** and fade-ins.
* Use **micro-interactions** on buttons and cards.
* Ensure accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA).
* Include **lazy loading** for large images and video content.
