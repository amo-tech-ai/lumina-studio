Responsive behaviour

On tablet: sidebar collapses to icon-only, charts stack two per row, cards shrink but maintain readable content

On mobile: sidebar becomes bottom nav or hamburger menu, top KPI cards become horizontal scroll or vertical list, main chart becomes full width, supporting widgets hidden or shown via toggle

Styling & Image Usage

Use our brand palette: primary accent colour, neutral greys for background and cards, whitespace to separate sections

Typography: Headings large and bold for clarity, body text comfortable reading size, ensure contrast for accessibility

Images: Use high-quality hero/background images only where needed (e.g., hero on detail page), cards should use simple icons or small graphics rather than full photos for faster load and clarity

Charts: use clean, modern line/bar/area visuals, minimal gridlines, clear legends, interactive hover states
