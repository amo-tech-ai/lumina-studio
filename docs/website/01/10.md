📱 Responsive & UX Considerations

Desktop: Multi-column grids, two-column sections, hover effects on cards, large hero visuals.

Tablet: Adjust to two-column or stacked layout, ensure images scale, cards stack if needed.

Mobile: Single column flow, hero image still full width, nav sticky if needed, cards vertical, form easily accessible, CTA buttons full-width.

Ensure fast load, lazy-loading for large images, clear CTA buttons, accessible font sizes, contrast for readability.

Provide reassuring micro-interactions: hover on cards, scroll reveal animations, consistent spacing.
