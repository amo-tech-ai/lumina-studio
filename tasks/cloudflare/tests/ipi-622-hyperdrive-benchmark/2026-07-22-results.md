## Interpretation (not facts)

These are judgments for **IPI-623** / **IPI-624**, not additional measurements:

1. Hyperdrive fresh and Data API are roughly on par for simple reads from a Worker (P50 43 vs 51 ms). That does **not** clearly justify a separate cached-Hyperdrive-config comparison under the ticket’s own bar.
2. Smart Placement showed no clear win in this synthetic 40-request burst; one route was slightly worse at P50. Do **not** enable Placement by default from this evidence alone.
3. Suggested *starting* alert thresholds for IPI-624 (P95 + headroom, empty-table synthetic run): Hyperdrive simple query P95 alert > 150 ms; insert+select P95 alert > 250 ms. Revisit after a real Mastra workload lands on Hyperdrive.
